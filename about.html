About Me

I work in Learning & Development and instructional design, using mainly open-source ecosystems to focus on how people learn and how knowledge can be structured effectively.

My background is in bilingual education; teaching history in both English & Dutch. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in both Education & History and have also taught English in Japan to all ages.

Remote & Global Ambition

My goal is to design effective learning experiences from anywhere in the world. I am currently transitioning towards remote-first work and building a lifestyle that allows me to work internationally.

I have a strong, long-term interest in establishing Japan as a base for my remote work and continued exploration of global education workflows.
